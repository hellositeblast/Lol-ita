// 001 — About

New name.
Same obsession.

Adelaide-based DJ LOL-ITA is bringing a high-energy fusion of iconic electronic anthems and modern club heaters to the local scene. Seamlessly blending nostalgia with cutting-edge production, her sound spans from the gritty, explosive energy of 90s breakbeat and rave classics to infectious house vocals and driving electronic rhythms.

Currently making waves as part of the Your Shot 2026 lineup and gearing up to take over Thursdays at Adelaide's own Nevermind, LOL-ITA is a rising force to watch behind the decks.
